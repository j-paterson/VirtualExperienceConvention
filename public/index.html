About This Event

Come to UC Berkeley's Virtual Experience Convention to learn more about VR/AR. Experience the latest VR/AR products, hear from industry leaders, and network with like-minded individuals to get a glimpse of what the future with VR & AR is going to look like! This is a great opportunity to join engineers, designers, entrepreneurs and visionaries in exploring the rapidly changing virtual / augmented reality industry.

Learn more about Virtual Reality

Virtual Reality at Berkeley is a student organization at UC Berkeley. We have partnered with Berkeley Robotics, Human Engineering and the Center for Augmented Cognition to host 15 different projects across multiple disciplines. Project applications welcome at the start of each semester!
