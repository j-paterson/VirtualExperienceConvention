About This Event

Virtual Experience Convention aims to connect industry leaders to young enthusiasts and entrepreneurs. Join engineers, designers, and visionaries to explore the rapidly changing virtual / augmented reality industry. Experience the latest VR/AR products, hear from industry leaders, and network with like-minded individuals.

Learn more about VR!

Virtual Reality at Berkeley is a student organization at UC Berkeley. We have partnered with Berkeley Robotics, Human Engineering and the Center for Augmented Cognition to host 15 different projects across multiple disciplines. General meetings are hosted weekly, Mondays 5-7 in Jacobs 310.
